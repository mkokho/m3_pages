# Cards

Source: https://m3.material.io/components/cards/overview

Cards display content and actions about a single subject

- Use cards to contain related elements
- Three variants: 

  elevated, 

  filled, 

  outlined
- Contents can include anything from images to headlines, supporting text, 

  buttons, and 

  lists
- Can also contain other components
- Cards have flexible 

  layouts and dimensions based on their contents
